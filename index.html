Reveal Yourself, Reflection!



This piece features a self-portrait of me looking at my drag persona, Sailor Claire.
This is intended to be looked at any angle, and plays into the idea of the "reflection world."
Basically, a world that shows us our true reflections. Sailor Claire is basically who my reflection is,
and everyone else has their own reflection that relates to who they truly are on the inside.
What do you think your reflection would look like?

Faces I Faintly Recognize



A piece of art I created for my Ways of Knowing Class.
I created it in remembrance of people in my life who, for whatever reason,
aren't really in it anymore. I see their ghosts all the time through
people who look exactly like them, and even though it's not them
deep down inside my heart I wish it was.

I put this in a presentation and I got a B- on it. To be fair, I shouldn't
have told the story about my old friends and the dog who would not stop
pooping in the parking lot. I think that professor just didn't like me cause
I caught him laughing at it, and I probably had the most class engagement
but that's besides the point and now I'm yapping.
